START YOUR JOURNY IN SHEGAON

The place where Sri Gajanan Maharaj lived is called Pandharpur in Vidarbha . A grand temple has been built on the Maharaja's burial site and its arrangements are being looked after by the 'Gajanan Maharaj Sansthan' there. Accommodation is provided at a low rate in the 'Bhakt Niwas' of the Sansthan there. There are arrangements of 700 rooms and during peak hours an additional 5000 beds are also provided. Different types of accommodation are available here according to the preferences of the devotees who come here. The same institute has built the tourist spot Anandsagar, Shegaon, out of about 350 acres of land, currently on 120 acres of land and it is very popular among tourists. Its expansion work is progressing very well. to Shegaon-On Balapur road, about 2.5 km from Shegaon. is at a distance. There is also a devotee residence at 'Anand Sagar Visava' nearby , where accommodation and food are also available.
